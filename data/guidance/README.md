# Source documents

This folder holds the guidance documents the assistant searches over.

The two PDFs included here are **genuine, publicly available guidance published
by the NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA)**, downloaded from
[nhsbsa.nhs.uk](https://www.nhsbsa.nhs.uk):

- `nhsbsa-hrt-ppc-guidance.pdf` — Guidance on the Hormone Replacement Therapy
  prescription prepayment certificate (HRT PPC), Feb 2024.
- `nhsbsa-maternity-exemption-guidance.pdf` — NHS maternity exemption
  certificate guidance, Feb 2024.

They are included only as realistic sample data to demonstrate the assistant.
All copyright remains with the NHSBSA / Crown. This project is an independent
prototype and is **not affiliated with or endorsed by the NHSBSA**.

To use your own documents, simply drop additional PDFs into this folder and
re-run `python ingest/build_index.py`.
